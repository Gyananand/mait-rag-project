The following steps are to be followed for signing of the registration chart by the students.
Regular student who has no back paper/s in any of the odd Semester is not required to do anything, the institute/department will take care of the same.
Passed out /Reappear/Year Back/Student having back paper/s in any of the odd Semester has to pay a reappear fees @ Rs. 1200/- per paper for a student per Semester.
The reappear fees only be paid through online mode using the following process/steps: Open the Institute website (
https://mait.ac.in
) ----> Click Reappear Fees       Fill your details and pay the complete Reappear fees as mentioned against your name.
OR
Click directly the link below to pay re-appear fees :
https://app.edumarshal.com/PayInstantFee?guid=4380bc59-941c-4bcc-9ba6-fada1054cebc
Reappear fee if paid in partial will not be accepted at all.
Such students may kindly verify the amount to be paid as reappear fees from the Reappear details uploaded on the MAIT website. After submitting the required reappear fees, the student concerned will send the proof of the payment made to their respective Coordinator/Group Counselor through WhatsApp or e-mail then only the Coordinator/Group counselor will confirm/approve his or her candidature. After completion of the reappear data file provided through google sheet, the respective Departmental RC Coordinator will submit the same to their respective Head of the department.
All the HODs are requested to cross verify the Reappear data file complete in all respect such as submission of reappear fees, remarks against non-submission of reappear fees etc, latest by October 24 (4:00 pm)), 2025.
