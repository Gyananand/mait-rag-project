Introduction 
 
The expert session on "How to Publish Your Research Work in High Impact Journals" was 
conducted by Prof. Sanjeev Gupta from the Central University of Himachal Pradesh, 
Dharamshala. 
 
The session was started with floral welcome to Prof. Sanjeev Gupta. Prof. Gupta shared 
valuable insights and practical strategies for publishing research work in high impact 
journals. Prof. Gupta emphasized the importance of understanding the impact factor and 
how to identify relevant high-impact journals in one's field. He also highlighted the 
significance of structuring a research paper effectively, including the abstract, introduction, 
methodology, results, and discussion. 
 
 Prof. Gupta stressed the need for a well-defined research question that aligns with current 
trends and gaps in the field. He also provided insights into the peer review system, 
including how to handle reviewer comments and revisions. Participants engaged in a lively 
Q&A session, seeking clarification on various aspects of publishing in high-impact 
journals. Prof. Gupta's expertise and insights provided valuable guidance for researchers 
aiming to publish their work in high-impact journals 
 
The session was well-received, and it was ended with the words of gratitude and thanks by 
Prof.  Harbhajan Bansal, Dean (Management) to the guest and all Participants.
