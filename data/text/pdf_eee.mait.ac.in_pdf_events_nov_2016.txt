One Day National Seminar on SMART GRID TECHNOLOGY 
 
The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering of Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology 
organized a One-day National Seminar on “SMART GRID TECHNOLOGY” on Nov. 27, 2016. It 
was successfully conducted by the Convener, Dr. Rajveer Mittal, HOD, EEE Department and 
attended by over 60 student delegates, more than 30 Assistant Professors & Associate Professors and 
4 Professors of eminence.  
 
          
 
 
The main objective of this seminar was to confer and propose the latest trends and advancements in 
Electrical Power System for the technical advancement of undergraduates/post graduates and faculty 
members. The seminar provided a platform for interactive sessions by experienced Researchers and 
Professionals, in Electrical Power, Energy and Control System. The eminent speakers from leading 
Academic Institutions and Research Organizations had been invited for expressing innovative 
technical ideas.
