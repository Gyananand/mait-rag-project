EEE Department 
Mock Interview Report 
 
EEE department conducted the mock interviews for final year students in the department. 
Approximately 20 students per day were interviewed. 
The mock interviews were coordinated by Dr. Monika Gupta (HOD, EEE) and Training 
& Placement coordinator Mr. Ashok Goyal, Assistant Professor (EEE). 
Purpose of the Mock Interviews: To simulate real interview environments for students and 
provide constructive feedback on their performance. The goal was to help students identify 
strengths and areas for improvement in preparation for actual job interviews. 
Methodology: 
Interviews were conducted by a panel of experts from the EEE department and Career  
Development Cell (MAIT). The panel of Experts and the schedule of mock interviews is given 
below.

Summary of Results 
Most students demonstrated good technical knowledge but showed varying levels of 
confidence and communication skills. Common issues included hesitation in answering 
behavioural questions and lack of concise responses. 
Feedback from Students: 
Most students expressed that the mock interviews were a valuable experience that helped them 
understand the interview process better.  
Many appreciated the opportunity to practice answering variety of questions, including 
technical and behavioural in a realistic setting, which boosted their confidence.  
Constructive feedback from interviewers helped students identify their strengths and areas for 
improvement. 
Students suggested to conduct more mock interviews on a regular basis to gain further practice.
