Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Department of Management organized one day 
industrial visit on 15th March 2024 to National Small Industries Corporation ( Technical Service 
Center) Incubator . The visit was organized for 46 MBA students with objective to develop 
entrepreneurial skills in budding mangers and to sensitize students on various entrepreneurship 
schemes provided by central and state Government. The students were accompanied by four 
faculty members  Dr Sukhvinder Singh , Mr Nishant Gaur, Mr Nakul Anand and Ms Neha 
Sharma from the Department of Management 
The industrial visit started with motivating lecture on entrepreneurship in which various benefits 
incentives and schemes for entrepreneur were discussed. Later the students visit LG center 
operated in collaboration with NSIC to encourage and promote entrepreneurship. 
Students also visit incubator center where they were exposed to various machines used to 
manufacturing tissue paper, disposables, cosmetics ,biscuits  etc 

During the visit students got a chance to learn and connect with industry experts and 
professionals, exchanging valuable insights and perspectives. At last student enjoyed 
refreshments provided by NSIC. The visit ended with nice group photograph for everlasting 
memories of learning and experience.
