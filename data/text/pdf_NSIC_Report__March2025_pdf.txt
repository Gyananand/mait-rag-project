Faculty Coordinators: Dr. Rajiv Jain, Dr. Ritu Gupta, Mr. Rajat Malik 
The Department of Management at Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology organized a one-day industrial visit 
to the National Small Industries Corporation (Technical Service Center) Incubator on March 17, 2025. The visit 
aimed to equip students with entrepreneurial skills and familiarize them with various government schemes 
supporting startups and small businesses. 
A total of 40 Students students participated in the visit, accompanied by faculty coordinators. The session began 
with an insightful lecture on entrepreneurship, covering various benefits, incentives, and schemes available for 
budding entrepreneurs. 
During the visit, students had the opportunity to explore the LG Center in collaboration with NSIC, which 
promotes entrepreneurial development. They also visited the Incubator Center, where they observed 
manufacturing units for tissue paper, disposables, cosmetics, and other products, gaining practical exposure to 
industry operations. 
Additionally, students interacted with industry professionals, exchanging valuable insights. The visit concluded 
with refreshments provided by NSIC and a group photograph capturing the experience.
