GEARING UP FOR THE FUTURE

Timings: 11:00 a.m. 
Venue: Online through MS Teams 
Teacher Coordinators: Mr. Nitin Walia, Dr. Anoop Kumar Gupta, Mr. Rajiv Jain, Dr. Ritu Gupta,

A Talk on Placements was organised for MBA studentsâ€™ batch 2019-2021 by the 
Department of Management, MBA, Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology on 2nd 
October 2020. The speakers for the session were six alumni of different batches of MBA 
namely Ms. Garima Tripathi (2008-10), Mr. Baljit Singh (2011-13), Ms. Sapna Dahiya 
(2013-15), Ms. Ritika Sethi (2015-17), Mr. Divij Bajaj (2016-18) & Mr. Shubham Gupta 
(2016-18)  
The talk started at 11:00 a.m. with a welcome speech by Dr. Amit Gupta (HOD) followed by 
motivational speech of our Director Prof. (Dr.) Neelam Sharma and inspirational words by 
Founder Chairman Dr. Nand Kishore Garg.  
The session started with the evolution of recruitment. The speakers shared their experiences 
and journey starting from giving out hard copy resumes back in the 1990s to build a personal 
brand in the 2020s. Further, they highlighted the importance of giving positive affirmations 
to self and power postures to build self- confidence. Along with that, they also gave some 
tips for various common yet difficult questions. The session was very interactive and the 
students participated very enthusiastically by asking some interesting queries regarding their 
career options in HR, Marketing and Finance as well as Entrepreneurship.  
It was concluded with vote of thanks by Dean Prof. S.S Deswal. 
A total of 97 participants took part in it and it was a two and half-hour session. It was such 
an interesting and insightful session that helped many students clear their doubts and queries 
regarding the future job opportunities.
