Report 
 
 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Organizer: Department of Management (MBA), Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology  
Date: 19th September, 2025 
Timings:  02:00 PM  
Venue:  Seminar Room, Block 7 
Convener: Prof. (Dr.) Harbhajan Bansal, Dean (Management) 
Faculty Coordinators: Dr. Ritu Gupta  
 
The session was convened by Prof. (Dr.) Harbhajan Bansal, Dean (Management), who welcomed the 
distinguished guest and resource person Dr. Neeraj Gupta, Associate Director – Security and 
Resiliency at Kyndryl with a sapling. Dr. Ritu Gupta started with welcome address and  underlined the 
importance of technology in today’s business environment and encouraged students to actively learn 
about AI-driven changes in management 
In his lecture, Dr. Neeraj Gupta elaborated on how Artificial Intelligence is revolutionizing 
management practices across industries. He explained its role in financial forecasting and analysis, 
where AI models can predict market trends and assist in better investment decisions. He highlighted the 
use of AI in customer relationship management, enabling companies to personalize services and 
improve customer satisfaction through data-driven insights. In human resource management, AI is 
being applied in talent acquisition, employee performance tracking, and workforce planning. He also 
discussed its growing significance in supply chain optimization, where AI helps in demand forecasting, 
inventory control, and logistics management. Furthermore, he explained how AI-based tools assist 
managers in strategic decision-making and risk management, making organizations more resilient and 
future-ready. 
A key highlight of the event was the interactive Q&A session, where MBA students enthusiastically 
asked questions about the opportunities and challenges of implementing AI, its ethical implications, 
and the career prospects it opens up for management graduates. Dr. Gupta answered each query in 
depth, blending academic knowledge with real-world experiences, which made the discussion highly 
engaging. 
The session concluded with a formal vote of thanks, extending gratitude to the convener, coordinator, 
resource person, and students. Overall, the lecture proved to be a highly enriching experience, offering 
MBA students a deeper understanding of how Artificial Intelligence is shaping modern management 
and preparing them to adapt to the evolving business landscape.
