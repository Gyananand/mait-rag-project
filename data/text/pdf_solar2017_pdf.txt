Two Day Technical Entrepreneurship Development Program  
 On  SOLAR TECHNOLOGY 
April 18-19, 2017 
 
 
Two Day workshop on Solar Technology was organized by EEE Department in the MAIT Campus 
during April 18-19, 2017. This workshop was conducted with the association of National Institute of 
Entrepreneurship & Small Business Development (NIESBUD), Govt. of India. The main aim of the 
workshop was to give the basic knowledge and utilization of solar power for domestic use. The main 
topics covered during this event were summarized as:   Introduction to Solar Energy, Components of 
solar system,  Basics of Solar System,  Roof top systems, Load Calculations,  Type of Structure & its 
choice, Basics of Installations, Series & Parallel Type connections, Correct Wiring & Assembly,  
Trouble Shooting Maintenance & Repairs, etc. More than 100 students were participated and 
benefited to get the exposure of Solar Technology. 
The workshop was planned under the kind guidance of Respected Dr. Nand Kishore Garg, Founder & 
Chief Advisor (MATES) and this initiative was facilitated by Mr. Vinay Dutta and Mr. Ravi Gupta 
(Consultant-IEDC). This program was organized under the supervision of Dr. Rajveer Mittal, HOD, 
EEE Department as the Program Incharge of this event. The Chairman Shri Prem Sagar Goyal, Joint 
Chairman Shri Mohan Garg, Director General, Dr. M.L. Goyal, Director, Prof. Neelam Sharma 
congratulate the department for the successful commencement of this workshop.
