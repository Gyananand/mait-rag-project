MAHARAJA AGRASEN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY 
DEPARTMENT OF MANAGEMENT 
Business Plan Competition 
 
Date: 4th & 5th December, 2020 
Venue:  Online MS Teams  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- 
A Business Plan Competition was organized for MBA Third Semester students, with a view 
to foster the spirit of entrepreneurship amongst them. It provided an integrative learning 
experience to the students as it was organised in four stages.  It commenced with intensive 
mentoring session by an external guest speaker followed by the Ideation stage. It was then 
followed by transition from Ideation stage to creating Business Plan. The third and qualifying 
round was held on 4th December, where 27 teams presented their Business ideas out of which 
9 teams reached the final stage, which was held on 5th December 2020. 
The first three prize winning teams were awarded cash prizes. Dr N. K.Garg, Founder 
Chairman and Chief Advisor, MATES graced the occasion with his words of encouragement 
to the students. Prof Dr. Neelam Sharma, Director MAIT a constant source of strength in all 
endeavours motivated students. Head of Department of Management (MBA), Dr. Amit Gupta 
congratulated winning teams and exhorted students to uphold spirit of entrepreneurship as our 
nation requires job creators rather than job seekers.
