Report on One Day Seminar  
 Role of NPTI in Power Sector 
on  
May 24, 2023 
One day seminar on “Role of NPTI in Power Sector” is organized by Electrical and Electronics 
Engineering department of Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Rohini, Delhi on May 24, 
2023. The programme started at 2pm with welcome address by Prof. Neelam Sharma, Director, 
MAIT. Who has emphasized the importance of renewable energies, especially, solar photovoltaic 
system and wind energy to meet the future challenges due to industrialization and global warming. 
Opening remarks and introduction of Dr. Vatsala Sharma, Dy Director, NPTI and her team to 
Director, MAIT is given by Prof. Rajveer Mittal, HOD, EEE. 
 
Dr Vatsala Sharma and her team being felicitated by Prof. Neelam Sharam and Prof. Rajveer Mittal  
Hundreds of students of EEE department and faculty member enthusiastically participated in the 
seminar. The students have raised number of queries related to carrier opportunities and industrial 
training to NPTI, team. 
The key points of the talk delivered are as, 
 
 Role of Electrical Engineers at NPTI. 
 NPTI and the Renewable Energies 
 Various Courses offered to undergraduates. 
 Various short term training programs offered to B. Tech Students 
 Workshops offered to engineering teacher fraternity.
