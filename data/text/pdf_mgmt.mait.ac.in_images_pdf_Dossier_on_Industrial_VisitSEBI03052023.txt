The objectives of the industrial visit to SEBI were 
1) To understand Demat and trading  account opening procedure.

3) To understand securities market in India

Part 1 
 
Aim of the event: 
 
The aim of the visit was to sensitize students on the functioning of securities market in India 
and to make students aware on the Demat and trading account opening procedure. The 
objective was to aware students on role and functions of Securities and Exchange Board of 
India . 
 
Part 2 
 
Abstract: 
 
Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology Department of Management organize visit to 
Securities and Exchange Board of India on 03rd May 2023 for MBA – IV semester students. 
Faculty members Dr Sukhvinder Singh and Mr Nishant Gaur accompanied 35 students on the 
visit to SEBI premises at SEBI’s Northern Regional Office at Kidwai Nagar New Delhi. 
 
The event started with basic introduction about SEBI followed by Demat and trading account 
opening guidelines and procedure. This session was conducted by senior officials from SEBI. 
The speaker emphasized on various frauds to which the investors are exposed while investing 
through different channels. The particular section of the presentation focused on DO’s and 
Don’ts while investing in securities market. They also deliberated on reading all the 
guidelines and instructions thoroughly before signing any documents or undertakings. Also to 
make session interactive, speaker asked various questions during presentation. 
 
Later students were shown videos and documentary made by SEBI for investor awareness. 
Students were severed with light refreshment at the end of the program. 
  
Part 3 
 
Conclusion 
 
The visit was a great source of learning for students as  the speakers shared their  expertise on 
the topic . Students also got an great opportunity to clear several doubts regarding  Demat and 
trading accounts, functioning of SEBI and IPO listing, Malpractices in stock  trading, Career 
opportunities at SEBI etc. The visit ended with light refreshment and group photo session.
