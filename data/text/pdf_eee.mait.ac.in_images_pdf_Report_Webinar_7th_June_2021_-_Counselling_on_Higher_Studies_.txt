Webinar on Counselling for Higher Studies 
 
The EEE Dept. of MAIT with IEEE MAIT hosted a session on Counselling on Higher Studies on 7th       
June 2021 to guide the students on their career and on the various opportunities which lie ahead.The guest 
of honor for the event was Sachin Srivastava, Carneige Mellon University, U.S.A. The event began with 
the unwavering and encouraging speech of the Director MAIT, Prof Neelam Sharma and Dean Academics 
Prof S.S.Deswal. The session was interactive with the participation of both the speaker and attendees. The 
speaker addressed all the queries and answered them warmly. The event was concluded with the vote of 
thanks by Prof. Rajveer Mittal HoD EEE. The event was further honored to have faculties and HODs of 
MAIT. 
 
 
 
  
Over 160+ participants registered for the webinar from the most far-reaching parts of the country 
and several prestigious colleges to gather knowledge on Higher Studies. Maharaja Agrasen 
Institute of Technology had the largest share of participants, with students from all years and 
disciplines actively taking part in the event. 
 They were given various guidance for their higher studies and also received career counseling 
from the expertise present in the event.
