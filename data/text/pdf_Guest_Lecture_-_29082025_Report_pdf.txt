MAHARAJA AGRASEN 
INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY 
 
 
Date: 29 August 2025 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Guest Lecture on Smart Entrepreneurship: Succeeding in the Age of AI and Innovation 
 
Organized By: Department of Management, MAIT 
 
Faculty Coordinators: Dr. Sanjeev Kumar and Dr. Anoop Kumar Gupta 
 
 
 
 
INTRODUCTION: 
With the inspiration of our Founder Chairman Dr. Nand Kishore Garg, the Department of 
Management of MAIT organized a guest lecture on the topic “Smart Entrepreneurship: 
Succeeding in the age of AI and Innovation”.  
 
The session aimed at inspiring and guiding students about the dynamic world of 
entrepreneurship in the modern era, with a special focus on the role of innovation and artificial 
intelligence in shaping business strategies. 
 

 
GUEST SPEAKERS: 
The event was graced by two eminent personalities: 
 
Prof. Dr. Sanjiva Shankar Dubey – A distinguished academician and former global 
executive with over four decades of impact in academia and industry. He has held 
leadership roles at BIMTECH, IIIT-Delhi, and several IIMs, besides a notable corporate 
career with IBM, Raffles Solutions Singapore and Tata Steel. Dr. Dubey is also the 
author of 16 books on IT Management, Strategy, and Innovation. 
 
 
Mr. Ravi Kumar Gupta – A renowned expert in energy, entrepreneurship and 
employability, he has more than 20 years of experience in mentoring youth, startups 
and women groups. He has trained over 1,25,000 individuals, facilitated employment 
opportunities and supported small enterprises through government programs. His 
association with institutions such as NIESBUD, NSIC and QCI has further amplified 
his contributions. 
 
 
KEY HIGHLIGHTS OF THE LECTURE: 
Both the distinguished speakers shared valuable insights on entrepreneurship in today’s rapidly 
changing environment. 
 
 
Prof. Dr. Sanjiva Shankar Dubey emphasized the importance of innovation and 
adaptability in the entrepreneurial journey. He discussed how AI is reshaping 
industries, creating new opportunities and challenging existing business models. His 
words motivated students to think critically, embrace technology and prepare 
themselves for leadership in the future economy. 
 
 
Mr. Ravi Kumar Gupta spoke extensively about employability and grassroots 
entrepreneurship. He highlighted the role of startups and self-reliance in strengthening 
the economy and shared his experiences of mentoring thousands of young 
entrepreneurs. His inspiring journey encouraged students to take initiative, leverage 
available resources and build sustainable ventures. 
 
 
CONCLUSION: 
The session proved to be an enriching experience for the students. Both speakers not only 
shared their expertise but also motivated the audience with their real-life experiences. Their 
words of encouragement instilled confidence among students to pursue entrepreneurship with 
creativity, innovation and resilience. 
 
On behalf of the organizing team and all the attendees, heartfelt gratitude was extended to Prof. 
Dr. Sanjiva Shankar Dubey and Mr. Ravi Kumar Gupta for their valuable time and guidance. 
The lecture concluded with optimism and inspiration, leaving the students eager to apply the 
learnings in their academic and professional journeys.
