Report 
 
Add-on Course on Basics of Stock Market 
 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Organiser: Department of Management, Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology  
Date: 15-16 February, 2024 
Timings:  10am -3 pm  
Venue:  Room No 742  
Faculty Coordinator: Dr.Ritu Gupta  
 
An  add- on course on “Basics of Stock Market in association with BSE” (10 hrs.) was organized for MBA 
and BBA students by the Department of Management, Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology from 15 -16 
February, 2024. The objective of the course was to educate the students on basic aspects of stock market and to 
ensure that participants in the stock market have adequate knowledge about the market operations to invest and 
trade independently and earn profits with minimum risk. Some of the major topics covered in the course are 
trading mechanism, technical and fundamental analysis of picking the right stock.Resource person for the course 
were industry expert and senior banking professional and faculty of BSE. 
Resource Person 1(Day1)---Dr. Daya Dhar Raj Srivastava -Senior Banking Professional 
(BSE) 
Resource Person 2(Day2)---Mr. Anupam Dixit – Sr. Faculty (BSE) 
 
A highly interactive query session was held after day end . The session was concluded by Vote of 
thanks by Dr. Ritu Gupta. 41 students enrolled the session and successfully completed the certification 
course. Overall, it was a great learning and valuable experience for all the students.
