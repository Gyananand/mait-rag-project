INTRODUCTION 
The 5-day certificate program, led by SIIC, IIT Kanpur, and backed by MSME, the course 
advanced business management aimed to provide participants insights into handling data 
effectively, optimizing operations, mastering supply chain management, understanding 
human behavior in organizational contexts, fostering entrepreneurship, driving innovation, 
and navigating international trades and business. Through immersive sessions, they 
acquired holistic knowledge to excel in diverse facets of modern business environments. 
 
DAY-BY-DAY ANALYSIS 
The program spanned five days and covered a diverse range of topics, including: 
 
DAY 1 
BUSINESS ANALYTICS FOR DECISION MAKING:  
Led by Dr. Poornima Gupta, Professor and Director PGDM at Great Lakes Institute of 
Management, participants delved into the realm of business analytics, learning how to 
leverage data-driven insights to make informed decisions, enhance operational efficiency, 
and gain a competitive edge in the marketplace. Additionally, they were introduced to 
advanced Excel functions and basic Python programming, providing them with practical 
tools to analyze data more effectively and automate repetitive tasks, thus strengthening 
their analytical capabilities. 
 
DAY 2 
OPERATIONS AND SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT: 
Under the guidance of Dr. Subrata Kumar Nandi, Professor and Deputy Director of FOSTIIMA 
Business School, participants embarked on a journey exploring the vital role of efficient 
operations and supply chain management in enhancing organizational performance and 
customer satisfaction. Through immersive sessions, they acquired strategies to streamline 
processes, optimize resources, and mitigate risks throughout the supply chain. Moreover, 
participants delved into advanced Excel functions, mastering techniques for solving 
transportation problems, thereby equipping themselves to tackle intricate logistical 
challenges and optimize distribution networks through insightful data-driven approaches. 
 
DAY 3 
INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS: 
Under the guidance of Dr. Rohit Mehtani, Professor and Consultant at the Indian Institute of 
Foreign Trade, participants embarked on an enlightening journey into the intricate realm of 
international trade and business. Delving into the nuances of the "Made in India" concept, 
they explored strategies for promoting indigenous products and brands on the global stage. 
Through immersive sessions, participants gained insights into global market trends, entry 
strategies, and expansion techniques. Additionally, they actively participated in customs and 
excise exercises, acquiring practical knowledge of import/export procedures, customs 
regulations, and trade compliance measures crucial for seamless international business 
transactions. 
 
 
 

DAY 4 
PEOPLE MANAGEMENT: 
Conducted by Dr. Tuheena Mukherjee, Assistant Professor at the Indian Institute of Foreign 
Trade, this day was dedicated to unraveling the intricate dynamics of organizational 
behavior, where participants analyzed human behavior and its impact on organizational 
effectiveness. They explored concepts such as motivation, leadership styles, communication 
patterns, and team dynamics. Additionally, participants learned strategies for effective 
people management, performance evaluation, and fostering a positive organizational 
culture. Through activities and interactive discussions, they gained practical insights into 
managing human resources and enhancing organizational performance. 
 
 
DAY 5 
ENTREPRENEURSHIP AND INNOVATION: 
Facilitated by Mr. Sanjay Dhar, Co-Founder and Director of Business Operations at 
'Manodayam,' an AI-driven platform for mental and emotional health, the final day of the 
program delved into the practical aspects of entrepreneurship, including strategies for 
securing investment and managing risks associated with starting a new business. 
Participants learned techniques for pitching their ideas to investors, crafting compelling 
business plans, and mitigating potential risks through effective planning and forecasting. 
Moreover, they explored the importance of building an efficient and experienced team, 
understanding how a skilled workforce contributes to the success and sustainability of a 
startup. Through interactive sessions and case studies, participants gained valuable insights 
into the entrepreneurial journey and learned strategies for overcoming challenges and 
maximizing opportunities in the competitive business landscape. 
 
 
 
 

 
 
 
 
 
 

CONCLUSION 
Over the course of five days, participants engaged in a dynamic learning journey led by 
experts from esteemed institutions and industries. They delved into diverse topics, including 
data analysis, supply chain optimization, global trade dynamics, effective people 
management, and entrepreneurial strategies. Throughout the program, students 
appreciated the hands-on approach, finding the content highly relevant to the challenges 
faced by MSMEs. Moreover, they valued the opportunity to network with peers, enhancing 
their professional connections and collaboration potential. Ultimately, the training 
successfully achieved its objectives, equipping participants with practical skills and valuable 
insights to navigate the complexities of the business world with confidence and agility.
