FORM B 
Objective: 
Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Rohini, Delhi organized its 4th National Seminar on 
“Emerging Paradigms in Management: Trends, Challenges and Opportunities (Building 
Sustainable Roadmap for Future) on May 26, 2023 aimed at raising awareness to the youth on 
sustainability issues for the long-term survival. The objective was to sensitize youth on the deeper 
understanding of global realities on sustainability and issues faced by global community. Also, 
to grapple the implications of the current situation with a myriad of terms and concepts to express 
a vast array of perspectives, priorities and approaches to address the critical global environmental 
issues. 
Abstract:  
The 4th National Seminar on Emerging Paradigms in Management, organized by Maharaja 
Agrasen Institute of Technology, focused on building a sustainable roadmap for the future. The 
seminar witnessed active participation of 350 students from MBA and BBA, faculty members, 
and industry professionals. Divided into three sessions - Inaugural Session, Technical Session 1, 
and Technical Session 2 (Valedictory Session) - the event covered various topics such as 
sustainable development goals, environmental impact, entrepreneurship, and innovation. 
Eminent speakers shared valuable insights and highlighted the importance of adopting sustainable 
practices in business. The seminar successfully provided a platform for knowledge exchange and 
learning, leaving a positive impact on the participants.  

4 
 
Conclusion 
In conclusion, the 4th National Seminar on Emerging Paradigms in Management, organized by 
the Department of Management at Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, was a successful 
event that brought together students, faculty, and industry experts to explore the trends, 
challenges, and opportunities in building a sustainable future. The seminar featured informative 
sessions by renowned speakers, fostering knowledge and enthusiasm among the participants. 
Overall, the seminar proved to be an enriching experience, contributing to the understanding of 
sustainability and its importance in the field of management.
